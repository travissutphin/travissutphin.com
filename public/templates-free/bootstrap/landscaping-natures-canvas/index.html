Our Process

Your Journey to Paradise

From first contact to final reveal, here's how we bring your outdoor vision to life.

Consultation & Vision

We start by listening. During our initial consultation, we learn about your lifestyle, preferences, and dreams for your outdoor space. We discuss your budget, timeline, and any challenges your property presents.

Design & Planning

Our designers create a custom plan that brings your vision to life. You'll receive detailed 3D renderings, plant selections, and material choices. We refine the design together until it's perfect.

Approval & Scheduling

Once you approve the design, we handle all necessary permits and schedule your project. You'll receive a detailed timeline and know exactly what to expect at each phase.

Installation & Creation

Our skilled team brings your design to life with precision and care. We keep you updated throughout the process and ensure minimal disruption to your daily routine.

Final Reveal & Care

We walk you through your transformed space, explaining care instructions for all plants and features. Plus, we offer ongoing maintenance services to keep your landscape thriving.
